mCart is an online shoppig application that helps the users 
to purchase various products.This application allows users to log 
in for purchasing mobiles,books,electronics devices etc.They can 
select the products and add them into the cart.Once the selection 
is done,they can go to the cart page for payment where they can 
select payment mode and checkout the purchase. They can can search
for product,filter the products list based on the type of products.
